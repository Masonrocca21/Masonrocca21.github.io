About

Hi, I’m Christian Colombo: welcome to my personal website. Here, you’ll get a glimpse into my world through the projects I’ve created. Each one reflects the way I think, design, and bring ideas to life. They’re not just games: they’re pieces of me. I'm passionate about game design and how interactive experiences can tell stories, challenge players, and create emotion. I love pushing myself with new ideas, experimenting with different mechanics, and learning as much as I can along the way. Whether you're here out of curiosity or looking for inspiration, I hope you enjoy exploring what I’ve built.

I’ve always been fascinated by how games can tell stories, challenge the mind, and bring people together.
Ever since I was a kid, I loved thinking about how to make others have fun, whether it was inventing games at home or organizing activities at the local youth center.
That passion naturally grew into an interest in game design during my studies, where I discovered how powerful interactive experiences can be when crafted with care.
Over time, I started building small prototypes, and refining my voice as a designer: always learning, always experimenting.

Il mio approccio

1. Design & Documentazione

Analizzo l'idea e la strutturo in documenti chiari (GDD, LDD) per definire la visione e le meccaniche di base.

2. Prototipazione & Iterazione

Sviluppo rapidamente prototipi giocabili per testare il "fun factor". I dati e il feedback guidano ogni miglioramento.

3. Rifinitura & Bilanciamento

Una volta consolidate le meccaniche, mi concentro sul bilanciamento, sulla user experience e sulla pulizia del codice.
